Utility Clarification Statement - ZacrocoZ (ZRZ)

ZRZ is a utility token designed exclusively to power the ZacrocoZ ecosystem. It does not constitute a financial security, equity, or investment instrument.

What ZRZ Is Not

Holding ZRZ does not confer any of the following rights:

  • Ownership in ZacrocoZ or any affiliated entity
  • Shares, stocks, or bonds
  • Profit-sharing or dividend entitlement
  • Voting rights in corporate structures unrelated to ZRZ governance
  • Any claim to revenue, equity, or legal ownership of the platform

ZRZ is strictly a functional digital asset within the ecosystem.

What ZRZ Provides

ZRZ empowers participants to engage with the ecosystem through utility-focused functionalities, including:

  • Platform Access: Unlock tools, dashboards, and ecosystem services
  • Governance Participation: Submit proposals, vote, and engage in structured DAO frameworks
  • Staking & Rewards: Participate in liquidity, staking programs, and incentive mechanisms
  • Ecosystem Interaction: Support partnerships, integrations, and collaborative initiatives

Regulatory Responsibility

Participants are responsible for ensuring compliance with all local laws and regulations regarding digital asset acquisition and usage. ZacrocoZ provides transparent, utility-driven access, but it is not an investment vehicle.

Key Takeaway

ZRZ is a participation and utility token, designed to:

  • Enable access to ecosystem services
  • Support governance and community engagement
  • Facilitate staking and incentive programs
  • Ensure transparent, structured, and long-term ecosystem utility

It is not a security, equity, or financial instrument, and any decisions regarding ZRZ acquisition must be made with awareness of its utility-only nature.
