Frequently Asked Questions?

What is ZacrocoZ (ZRZ)?
Answer:

ZacrocoZ is a purpose-built blockchain ecosystem powered by the ZRZ utility token. ZRZ enables participants to:

  • Access platform tools and AI-driven dashboards
  • Participate in structured governance and voting
  • Stake tokens and earn rewards
  • Contribute to ecosystem development and strategic programs

ZRZ is not speculative — it is designed to support long-term utility, governance, and ecosystem growth.

What is the Total Supply and Token Standard?
Answer:
  • Total Supply: 110,000,000 ZRZ
  • Blockchain: BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Official Contract Address 0x4cFa773Df72A3cCB89cb643Ad4c2A9b9F1692F5A 🔗
  • Minting: Permanently disabled
  • Burn: Optional public burn for voluntary reduction

Key Point: The entire supply was minted once at deployment. There is no inflation, no tax, and no upgrade proxy. The contract is transparent, final, and verifiable on-chain.

How does liquidity work?
Answer:

Liquidity deployment is structured and phased, not instantaneous:

  • Phase 1: 20,000,000 ZRZ – Locked immediately for 3 years
  • Phase 2: 10,000,000 ZRZ – Locked 3 years at deployment
  • Phase 3: 10,000,000 ZRZ – Locked 3 years at deployment
  • Phase 4: 10,000,000 ZRZ – Locked 3 years at deployment
Highlights:
  • Liquidity expands gradually to reduce market shock
  • Each phase is locked individually
  • All LP deployments are on-chain verifiable
  • Remaining 60M tokens are allocated for development, ecosystem, marketing, and reserve
